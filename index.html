What Is Color Therapy

color therapy, also known as chromotherapy, is a from of therapy that uses color and light to treat certain mental and physical health conditions. we can trace this from of therapy back to the ancient Egyptians. They made use of sun-filled rooms with colored glasses for therapeutic purposes.

Types of Color Therapy

in color therapy, different colors are thought to be able to treat various conditions. Color therapists blieve that a lack of a particular color in your body might be responsible for your living condition. here's a list of the most commom colors used during color therapy and how we can use them.

  • Red:Red is used to energize or invigorate a person who might be felling tired or down. However, red may also trigger people who might already be tense, which is why it's used carefully in color therapy.

  • Blue:Chromatherapists use blue to treat and pain. Darker shades of blue are also thought to have sedative properties and may be helpful for people who experience insomnia or other sleeping disorders.

  • Green: Green is the color to nature, and according to chromatherapists, it can help relieve stress and relax a person.

  • Yellow: Yellow can be used to improve your mood and make you happy and optimistic.

  • Orange: Orange, much like yellow, can be used to elicit happy emotions from people. The bright warm color is also thought to be able to stimulate appetite and mental ativity.
